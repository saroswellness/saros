Biriyani Premix

Approx. Weight: 50g (Serves 2–3) / 100g (Serves 4–5)

Shelf Life: 5 months

Storage: Keep refrigerated. Store airtight after opening. Store in an airtight container for best results.

Marinate chicken / paneer / mixed vegetables / soya with a small portion of Saro’s Premix, yoghurt, and salt. Keep aside for 15 minutes. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and sauté freshly chopped onions until golden. Add chopped tomatoes, salt, and the remaining premix. Sauté for 2 minutes. Add the marinated chicken/ paneer / vegetables / soya and sauté for 2–3 minutes. Cover and cook until done. Add cooked basmati or jeerakasala rice (cooked with salt). Mix gently, cover, and cook on low flame (dum) for 5 minutes. For a Kashmiri Pulao twist, add freshly chopped fruits and nuts such as pomegranate, apple, grapes, raisins, almonds, and cashews. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Sambar Premix

Approx. Weight: 50g / 100g

Shelf Life: 5 months

Storage: Keep refrigerated. Ensure the pack is tightly closed after use. Store in an airtight container for best results.

In a pressure cooker, mix Saro’s Premix with warm water, chopped vegetables, and salt. Pressure cook for 2 whistles. Open the lid and simmer until the sambar thickens to your desired consistency. Serve hot with rice, idli, dosa, or any of your favourite dishes. For a darker, richer sambar flavour, dry roast the premix until dark brown before cooking.

Chana Masala Premix

Approx. Weight: 50 grams (Serves 2–3 persons) / 100 grams (Serves 4–5 persons)

Shelf Life: 5 months

Storage Instructions: Keep refrigerated. Ensure the pack is tightly closed after use. Store in an airtight container for best results.

Heat oil in a pressure cooker and sauté freshly chopped onions and green chillies until soft. Add Saro’s Premix, water, salt, and soaked chickpeas (soaked for 6 hours). Mix well. Pressure cook until the first whistle. Then lower the flame and cook for 4 more whistles. Once the pressure is released, simmer until the gravy thickens to your desired consistency. Serve hot with poori, jeera rice, bhatura, chapathi, or rice.

Kuruma Premix

Approx. Weight: 50 grams (Serves 2–3 persons) / 100 grams (Serves 4–5 persons)

Shelf Life: 5 months

Storage Instructions: Keep refrigerated. Ensure the pack is tightly closed after use. Store in an airtight container for best results.

Heat ghee, butter, or oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions, green chillies, and your choice of vegetables / chicken / paneer for 2–3 minutes. Add Saro’s Premix along with salt and water. For a richer taste, add coconut milk or milk powder. Cover and cook until everything is well cooked and the gravy thickens. Garnish with fresh curry leaves or coriander leaves. Serve hot with ghee rice, appam, pathiri, chapathi, or rice.

Kadai Premix

Approx. Weight: 50 grams (Serves 2–3 persons) / 100 grams (Serves 4–5 persons)

Shelf Life: 5 months

Storage Instructions: Keep refrigerated. Ensure the pack is tightly closed after use. Store in an airtight container for best results.

Add Saro’s Premix and cook for 1 minute. Add a splash of warm water and cook on medium flame until everything is fully cooked. Stir occasionally and serve hot. Theeyal Premix

Approx. Weight: 50g (Serves 2–3) / 100g (Serves 4–5)

Shelf Life: 5 months

Storage: Keep refrigerated. Store airtight after opening. Store in an airtight container for best results.

Cooking Instructions: Heat 1 tbsp coconut oil and sauté 200-250 shallots for 3–4 minutes. Add 50g Saro's Theeyal Premix and 400–450ml water. Add salt to taste and bring to a boil. Simmer for 12–15 minutes until the shallots are tender and the gravy thickens. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Millet-Moringa Soup

Approx. Weight: 250g / 500g / 1kg (Servings vary)

Shelf Life: 1 month

Storage Instructions: Keep refrigerated. Ensure the pack is tightly closed after use. Store in an airtight container for best results.

Empty 1 sachet / 1.5 tbsp of Saro’s Millet–Moringa Soup into a cup or bowl. Add 1 cup of room-temperature water and mix well until smooth. Microwave for 1 minute 10 seconds or cook on stovetop for 2–3 minutes while stirring gently.

Serve hot and enjoy a warm, wholesome bowl of goodness anytime.

For a richer taste, add a splash of milk or coconut milk before cooking.
